ABOUT THE SwiftIQ GROUP

SWIFTIQ is a dynamic and innovative company that serves as a hub for various specialized mini companies. With a strong focus on delivering exceptional services and solutions, our diverse portfolio caters to the unique needs of different industries and individuals.

  • 1. AreyengSghela.
  • 2. Might Marketing Agency
  • 3. Swift Capital
  • 4. SwiftDevops

At SWIFTIQ, we strive for excellence in every endeavor. By combining the expertise of our mini companies, we offer a holistic range of services designed to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Whether you're a student seeking academic support, a business owner looking to enhance your brand, or an organization in need of reliable procurement solutions, SWIFTIQ is your partner for success.

AreyengSghela

AreyengSghela is your go-to destination for academic support and guidance. We understand the challenges faced by students in today's educational landscape, and our team of experienced professionals provides top-notch assistance. Whether it's helping with assignments, offering tuition services, or debugging codes, we specialize in Computer Science and related courses, ensuring that students receive the support they need to excel.

Mighty Marketing Agency

Mighty Marketing Agency is your creative partner for all things design and advertising. We specialize in crafting visually appealing and impactful designs that captivate your target audience.

SwiftDevops

SwiftDev is a mini company that offers comprehensive web and app development services. Our team of skilled professionals excels in consulting, designing, and developing websites, native apps, a nd cross-platform apps. With a focus on creating intuitive user experiences and robust functionalities, we leverage cutting-edge technologies to bring your ideas to life. Additionally, we provide network building, website maintenance, and hosting services, ensuring that your online presence is secure and optimized.

Swift Capital

Swift Capita is your trusted partner for procurement and subcontracting needs. We specialize in sourcing supplies and securing tenders for various industries. Our dedicated team is well-versed in the intricacies of the tendering process, ensuring that you have a competitive edge. Whether you need assistance with tender applications, supplier selection, or contract management, Swift Capita is committed to streamlining your procurement processes and maximizing your opportunities.
